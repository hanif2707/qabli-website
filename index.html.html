How to Make Qabli Pulao

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 500g lamb or beef
  • 2 medium carrots (julienned)
  • 1/2 cup raisins
  • 1 medium onion (sliced)
  • 4 cups water
  • 1/2 tsp ground cardamom
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Rinse the rice and soak it in water for 30 minutes.
  2. In a pot, heat oil and saut√© the onions until golden brown.
  3. Add the meat and cook until browned. Add spices, salt, and water. Simmer until the meat is tender.
  4. Remove the meat and set aside. Strain the broth and reserve it.
  5. In the same pot, fry the carrots until soft, then add the raisins and cook briefly.
  6. Layer the rice, meat, and broth in the pot. Cover and cook on low heat until the rice is fluffy.
  7. Serve with the carrot and raisin mixture on top.
